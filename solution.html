What Can We Do?

Informing students and citizens about human trafficking in schools and on the internet. Since social media is the fastest and best way to approach others about something, posting information of trafficking on the internet will catch more people's attention than on a paper.

Moreover, one of the biggest reasons people get lured into human trafficking, especially forced labor, is because they are willing to take any jobs with any conditions rather than to be unemployed with no income. Thus, providing employment opportunities for those in need will decrease the growth of human trafficking.

Furthermore, the government should focus on strengthening and enforcing laws against human trafficking. Investigating victims who get out of the human traffickers and the forced labor environment in order to understand more depth about the issue.
